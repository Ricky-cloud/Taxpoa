Help & Support

Got questions? We've got answers. Find solutions to common queries below.

How does the KRA PIN verification work? expand_more

Our service securely connects to KRA's official verification APIs in real-time. When you enter a PIN, we send an encrypted request and display the authenticated response directly from the KRA servers. We do not store any of your verification data, ensuring maximum privacy.

Is my data safe? expand_more

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission between your browser and our servers, as well as between our servers and the KRA APIs. Your sensitive information is never stored, logged, or shared.

What information is returned after a successful verification? expand_more

A successful verification returns the holder's full name, the PIN status (e.g., Active or Inactive), the entity type (Individual or Company), and the original registration date, as provided directly by the KRA database.
